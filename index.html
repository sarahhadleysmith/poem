with one fall
your shell shears away
a conchoidal fracture

your outside
white, splotched pale blue
like the rare intact glacier

your inside
rings of dark grey, darkening into
the melancholic
horror, terror, sublime
the glacier once the warming ocean has taken it and the ice mixes with the angry rumbling sea!

You were made from water, under greenhouse conditions, from carbon relieved from the air.

We were made from land, under icebox conditions, and carbon was forced into the air.

a city as it crumbles and is swallowed by sea, letting the vengeful ocean take it!
the horror, terror, sublime
a rising storm surge
emerging from a series of grey waves
our underbelly

like the rare intact glacier
perfect, clean light-steel
superficially

a world fractured
it will all cleave away
with one fall
